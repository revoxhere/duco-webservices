Create a new wallet

The username will also be your wallet address. It can consist of alphanumeric characters, numbers and underscores or dashes.

Choose a strong password and save it in a safe place.

Optional: setup a key that you will put in your miners so that other people can't mine on your account and mess with your Kolka efficiency drops. You can leave that field blank and enable it in the future in settings.

Please provide a real e-mail as it will be used to confirm exchanges and receive updates about your account. We will delete your account if the e-mail is invalid. We won't send spam.
